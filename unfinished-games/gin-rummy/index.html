Gin Rummy Rules

Gin Rummy is played with a standard 52-card deck. The objective is to collect cards into melds and have as little deadwood (unmatched cards) as possible.

Melds: A meld can be a sequence of 3+ cards of the same suit (run) or 3-4 cards of the same rank (set).

Gameplay: Each player gets 10 cards. On your turn, draw a card from the deck or discard pile, then discard one card. You can "knock" if your deadwood is 10 points or less.

Scoring: Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10 points. Aces are worth 1. Other cards are worth their face value.

Gin: When all your cards form melds with no deadwood, you can call "Gin" for a bonus.
