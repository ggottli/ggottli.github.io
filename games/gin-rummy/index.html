How to Play:

  • Draw: Click stock pile or discard pile to draw (adds 11th card).
  • Discard: Click a card in your hand to discard (if you have 11 cards).
  • Sort: Drag cards in your hand to rearrange them.
  • Melds: 3+ cards of same rank OR 3+ consecutive cards of same suit (Ace is low).
  • Knock: With 11 cards, if an optimal discard results in deadwood ‚â§ 10 pts, the "Knock" button appears. Clicking it auto-discards and knocks.
  • Gin: If optimal discard results in 0 deadwood, it's "Gin!".
  • Scoring: Face cards = 10pts, Aces = 1pt, Numbers = face value.
  • Bonuses: Gin = +25 points to knocker | Undercut (knocker has more deadwood than opponent) = +25 points to opponent.
