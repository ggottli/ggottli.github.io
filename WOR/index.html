Welcome to War of the Roses

Hosted by Pi Kappa Phi, this week is full of fun events working to raise awareness and funds for those living with disabilities. The contestants have worked hard at all the events and practicing their talents! Enjoy a dazzling display of performances while supporting a noble cause.

About the Event

War of the Roses is more than a talent show—it’s a celebration of empowerment, creativity, and community. All proceeds from this event go to The Ability Experience, an organization dedicated to raising funds and awareness for people with disabilities.

Enjoy performances ranging from music and dance to art, magic, and more. Each performer brings her unique spark to the stage, and we invite you to join us in celebrating their passion.
