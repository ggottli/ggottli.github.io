Welcome to War of the Roses

Hosted by Pi Kappa Phi, this event celebrates the talent and spirit of young women from our community. Enjoy a dazzling display of performances while supporting a noble cause.

About the Event

War of the Roses is more than a talent show—it’s a celebration of empowerment, creativity, and community. All proceeds from this event go to The Ability Experience, an organization dedicated to raising funds and awareness for people with disabilities.

Enjoy performances ranging from music and dance to art, comedy, and more. Each performer brings her unique spark to the stage, and we invite you to join us in celebrating their passion.
