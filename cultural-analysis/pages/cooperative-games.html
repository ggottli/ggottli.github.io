Types of Cooperative Gameplay

Fully Cooperative

All players work together against the game system, sharing a win or loss.

Examples: Pandemic, Forbidden Island

Hidden Traitor

Most players cooperate while one or more secretly work against the group.

Examples: Battlestar Galactica, Dead of Winter

Semi-Cooperative

Players must work together but ultimately compete for individual victory.

Examples: Cosmic Encounter, Archipelago

Growth of Cooperative Games

According to industry reports, sales of cooperative board games increased by 25% in a single year (2022).
